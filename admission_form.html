Terms and Conditions of Wazir Education Society also known as WES or Institute:

1. I undertake that it would be obligatory on my part to participate in all academic, co-curricular, and extracurricular practices inclusive of research, training, industrial visits, seminar, study tours, placements and other activities in our outside the Campus, organized/arranged by WES of the fee and charges prescribed for the purpose or otherwise as deemed fit by the WES from time to time.

2. I shall maintain a minimum of 90% Attendance in both Theory and Practical in all subjects and submit all Assignments, projects, and portfolios in time. If I am unable to comply with these norms I understand that I shall not be allowed to attend the class and/or be part of the evaluation process nor remain eligible to receive the course completion certificate.

3. In Case I am unable to maintain regular attendance then I am aware that my parents may be informed by email or phone regarding my absenteeism.

4. Passing all Tests and Examinations is mandatory to get a Certificate. Students have to submit a leave application giving valid reasons for absence from the classes to the concerned teacher

5. I shall ensure that my absence from classes for more than 10 days without prior information shall lead to cancellation of the admission and would attract re-admission formalities as may be decided on a case-to-case without any refund of fees.

6. I shall adhere to the prescribed Dress Code in the Institute and display my ID card at all times on the campus (if required).

7. I shall comply with the rules and regulations prescribed for the usage of Institute facilities and infrastructure viz. Library, Computer Lab, Workshop, etc.

8. I shall undertake to inform the changes in the Contact number or Address immediately to the Student Counselor /Reception Counter.

9. I shall not indulge in any unsocial, criminal or political activity, which does not conform to the rules and regulations of the Institute.

10. I shall refrain from the consumption of all tobacco products, alcohol, or chewing of Gutkha, inside and within 100 yards of the Institute.

11. Understand that ragging in any form is strictly prohibited in accordance, with the directions of the Honorable Supreme Court of India, I shall ensure that if I am found indulging in any kind of ragging the Institute may initiate prescribed legal action against me or I may be rusticated.

12. I shall comply with Institute policies regarding usage of lab/personal laptops on the Institute premises and refrain from installing using or copying any pirated software on personal laptops or Institute computers while in the Institute network.

13. I shall ensure thorough compliance with the Software License agreement by not using a pen drive in the Institute. As per Institute policy, I shall access my data through Google Drive, Dropbox or email.
